### Phase 1 (Anchored Turret) — What the Drowned Engine Can Do

**State in Phase 1**

* **Speed 0 (stationary)** on the dais
* **Immune to all damage**
* **No Legendary Resistance used in Phase 1**
* **Action economy:** **1 Action on its turn**, **1 Legendary Action per round**, **1 Lair Action at initiative 20**

---

## On its Turn (choose **one Action** each round)

### 1) **Harpoon Winch** *(only while Present/Dynamo pylon is active)*

* **Attack:** +8 to hit, range 60/180, 1 target
* **Hit:** **2d10+4** piercing/force (avg **15**)
* Target is **grappled** (escape **DC 16**)

### 2) **Guilt Fog** *(only while Past/Memorial pylon is active; Recharge 5–6)*

* **Area:** 30-ft cone
* **Save:** **DC 16 Con**
* **Fail:** **4d8** necrotic (avg **18**) and **can’t regain HP until start of its next turn**
* **Success:** half damage, no rider

### 3) **Boiler Vent** *(Recharge 5–6)*

* **Area:** 15-ft cone
* **Save:** **DC 16 Con**
* **Fail:** **4d8** fire (avg **18**) and **pushed 10 ft**
* **Success:** half damage, no push

### 4) **Time Shear** *(only while Future/Prism pylon is active; Recharge 4–6)*

* **Target:** 1 creature within 90 ft
* **Save:** **DC 15 Wis**
* **Fail:** **3d8** psychic (avg **13**) and **Slowed until end of its next turn**:

  * speed halved
  * no reactions
  * on its turn it can take **either** an action **or** a bonus action (not both)
* **Success:** half damage, no slow

### 5) **Slam** *(if someone is in reach; always available)*

* **Attack:** +9 to hit, reach 10 ft, 1 target
* **Hit:** **2d8+6** bludgeoning (avg **15**)

---

## Legendary Actions (Phase 1): **1 per round**

Use at the end of a PC’s turn.

### A) **Winch Pull** *(Present active; requires a grappled target)*

* Pull the grappled creature **20 ft** (no save)

### B) **Tail Tap** *(always)*

* 1 creature within 10 ft makes **DC 15 Str** save or is **knocked prone** (no damage)

### C) **Temporal Reversal** *(Future active)*

* When a pylon would lose a breakpoint, reduce the damage to that pylon by **10** (after modifiers)

---

## Lair Actions (Initiative 20, Phase 1): **1 per round**

Choose one option tied to an **active** pylon.

### Past active — **Undertow**

* Choose a 10-ft square; creatures there **DC 15 Str** save or **restrained** until start of next turn

### Present active — **Arc Flash**

* Up to 2 creatures **DC 15 Dex** save
* Fail: **2d6** lightning (avg **7**) and **no reactions** until next turn
* Success: half damage, keep reactions

### Future active — **Phase Flicker (Pylon Defense)**

* Choose one pylon: until next init 20 it gets **+2 AC** and its **Damage Threshold increases by 5**
