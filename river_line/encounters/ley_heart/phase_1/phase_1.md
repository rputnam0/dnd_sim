Phase 1: The Three Procedure Pylons (DM Quick Reference)
Goal: The boss is invulnerable until all 3 pylons are destroyed.
Core loop: Each pylon is Resistant until a PC performs its Procedure to make it Exposed for a short window, then the party burns it down.

Shared Pylon Rules (all three)
Pylon Stats
AC: 15
HP: 45
Damage Resistance: Resistant to all damage while not Exposed.
Exposed (simple)
While Exposed, the pylon loses its resistance and takes normal damage.
Duration: until the start of the exposing creature’s next turn.
Breakpoints
At 30 HP and 15 HP, the pylon triggers a Breakpoint Pulse (below).
At 0 HP, the pylon is destroyed (tether snaps; no explosion).
Breakpoint Pulse (all pylons)
Trigger: when the pylon is reduced to 30 HP or 15 HP
Area: creatures within 10 ft of that pylon
Save DC: 15 (save type varies by pylon)
Damage: 2d6 (damage type varies by pylon)
On fail: full damage + rider
On success: half damage, no rider

1) Past Pylon — Iron Memorial (Roll Call)
Theme: regret, names, rites; dog tags and rosters fused into iron.
Procedure: Final Roll Call
Action, within 5 ft
Check: DC 15 Performance or Persuasion or Religion
Success: pylon becomes Exposed (no resistance) until start of your next turn
Failure: no effect
Breakpoint Pulse: Memorial Pulse
Save: DC 15 Constitution
Damage: 2d6 necrotic
Fail rider: no reactions until the start of your next turn

2) Present Pylon — Arcane Dynamo (Safety Seal)
Theme: voltage, regulation, pressure; a humming regulator housing with an intact seal.
Procedure: Cut the Safety Seal
Action, within 5 ft
Check: DC 15 Thieves’ Tools or Arcana
Success: pylon becomes Exposed until start of your next turn
Failure: no effect
Breakpoint Pulse: Dynamo Pulse
Save: DC 15 Dexterity
Damage: 2d6 lightning
Fail rider: pushed 10 ft directly away from the pylon

3) Future Pylon — Crystal Prism (Sync Dial)
Theme: phasing, prediction, synchronization; an out-of-phase prism with a rotating etched dial.
Procedure: Set the Sync Dial
Action, within 30 ft and line of sight
Check: DC 15 Investigation or Insight
Success: pylon becomes Exposed until start of your next turn
Failure: no effect
Breakpoint Pulse: Prism Pulse
Save: DC 15 Wisdom
Damage: 2d6 psychic
Fail rider: disadvantage on your next attack before the end of your next turn

Running Notes (one-liners you can say at the table)
Before exposure: “Your blows spark and skid—this thing is shielded.”
On success: “The pylon stutters—it’s exposed! Hit it now!”
At 30/15: “It cracks and pulses—everyone close, save!”
